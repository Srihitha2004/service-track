about our page

ServiceTrack is an innovative web application designed to streamline vehicle service management and ensure timely maintenance. With features like tracking service histories, scheduling maintenance tasks, and automated reminders, ServiceTrack helps users maintain their vehicles efficiently and effectively.

Our Services

The maintenance and servicing of vehicles play a critical role in ensuring their longevity and safety, yet users often face challenges in keeping track of service schedules and records. This project, ServiceTrack, addresses these challenges by providing a web-based solution for efficient vehicle service management. The system allows users to seamlessly manage vehicle data, track service records, and receive timely service reminders. The user-friendly interface, designed using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, enhances accessibility while ensuring a smooth user experience.
